About me

My name is Andy Wegner.

I am the Vice President of Software Engineering at a mid-sized company leading a team of developers that spans the globe. I've also worked at a large manuafacturing company building software that covers pretty much every aspect of a product's life cycle: initial conception, design, manufacturing, transportation, field operations, warranty returns, repair and remanufacturing.

I was the community owner of Team Vipers for 5 years. Vipers was a gaming community, focused mainly around Team Fortress 2, but we dabbled in other games for brief periods of time. I helped the community prosper. I also built a lot of software to help me in managing the community.

I am active on Stack Overflow and the rest of the Stack Exchange network. I moderate Community Building and Hardware Recommendations. I've also built a few tools to help keep the low quality content off the Stack Exchange network.

My Projects

  • StackAPI A Python wrapper for the Stack Exchange API.
  • Zephyr - Vote Request Bot Zephyr is a headless chatbot that monitors chat rooms on the Stack Exchange network for vote requests from other users and posts them to a specified room.
  • IRVING IRVING is a system built to integrate directly with end user systems. Users know their data and they know the conditions that are abnormal and worth monitoring. IRVING allows users to set up a customized dashboard to monitor their important data points through multiple systems.
